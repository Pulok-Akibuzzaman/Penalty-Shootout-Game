About the Game

What is it?

A football penalty shootout game where you try to score as many goals as possible against an AI goalkeeper.

How to Play

  1. Enter your name and pick a country.
  2. Choose a shot direction: Left, Center, or Right.
  3. If the goalkeeper dives the other way, you score!
  4. If the goalkeeper guesses correctly, you miss.

Rules

  • You have 3 lives (misses allowed).
  • Each goal = +1 point.
  • After 3 misses the game is over.
  • Your highest score is saved automatically.

Controls

Click or tap one of the three direction buttons to shoot.
